Gleb Mareyev
Co-founder, CCO

Gleb entered music business in far 2000 when he founded his own band and a small sound recording studio. This studio was named "Metalhearts" and very soon Gleb with his partner started organizing concerts and promoting different young local groups. Metalhearts exists nowadays and it has held hundreds of events on several stages in different Russian cities.

Eugene Panaitov
Co-founder, CEO

Eugene joined Metalhearts in 2004 and quickly took hold of a significant role in concert managing process including advertising, public relations and marketing. Eugene's vast experience in modern IT science made the existence of tourageous.com project possible implementing a bunch of solutions for problems that Eugene and Gleb were facing while organizing concerts in Metalhearts.
